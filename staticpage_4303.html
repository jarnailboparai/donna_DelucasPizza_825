Meet Anthony

<a href="http://delucasbrickovenpizza.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Anthony.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-173 alignleft" style="border: 3px solid black;" alt="Anthony" src="http://delucasbrickovenpizza.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Anthony-300x199.jpg" width="300" height="199" /></a><span style="color: #000000;">Anthony Valinoti is the proprietor of DeLuca's Pizzeria Napoletana, AKA DeLuca's Brick Oven Pizza, in beautiful, downtown Hot Springs, Arkansas. A native of Brooklyn, NY,  Anthony, after living in several other cities, fell in love with Hot Springs and decided to make it his home. He found that he missed his home-town pizza, though, and opened his restaurant which is his passion. DeLuca was his grandfather's name which explains the origin of the name.</span>

<span style="color: #000000;">Located on historic Park Ave., DeLuca's Brick Oven Pizza is fast becoming a popular restaurant for locals and tourists alike. People say they are made to feel like family when they walk through the door.</span>

<span style="color: #000000;">Anthony is a stickler for quality. So be assured, he chooses only the finest ingredients for his<a href="http://delucasbrickovenpizza.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/photo-4.jpg"><img class="alignright size-medium wp-image-170" style="border: 3px solid black;" alt="photo 4" src="http://delucasbrickovenpizza.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/photo-4-200x300.jpg" width="200" height="300" /></a> specialty, Napoletana pizzas. He uses such items as hand-crushed tomatoes, fresh mozzarella cheese, parmigiana &amp; reggiano cheese, fresh basil and arugula, portabello and shitake mushrooms, extra virgin olive oil, Capocollo, smoked pork shoulder with Italian seasoning, a Busetto Brothers spicy sopressata, and other exceptional ingredients.</span>

<span style="color: #000000;">Interestingly, there is actually a rule-making board in Naples, Italy, that governs Napoletana pizza authenticity. It was while Anthony was living in Naples that he learned and trained in the art of making authentic Napoletana pizzas from master chefs, and, as a result, the ones he makes in his restaurant for his customers are truly special.</span>

<span style="color: #000000;">Arrange to have lunch or dinner at DeLuca's Brick Oven Pizza to experience what others are calling the "best pizza in Hot Springs."  The restaurant menu has a great beer and wine selection, as well as, home-made Italian desserts such as cannolis. You can order an espresso, too.</span>

<span style="color: #000000;">Anthony and his staff look forward to meeting you and being your hosts. The restaurant is closed on Tuesday and Wednesday and open the other days of the week. </span>

<span style="color: #000000;">The address is 407 Park Ave, Hot Springs, AR 71901. </span><span style="color: #000000;">Call them to order take-out.</span><span style="color: #000000;"> See you there. (Restaurant details are in the side-bar.)</span>
